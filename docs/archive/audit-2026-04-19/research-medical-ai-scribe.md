## Emerging 2025-2026 trends

1. **Multimodal vision** — Gemini + Ray-Ban Meta glasses = 98% accuracy on medication histories (vs 81% audio-only)
2. **Speaker diarisation** — 96-98% accuracy with 2-5 speakers; AssemblyAI 30% improvement in noise (2025)
3. **Patient-facing summaries** — Nuance + Abridge + Suki auto-draft after-visit handouts
4. **Fine-tuning** — DPO outperforms SFT for clinical reasoning; MedASR trained on 5000+ hrs specialty speech
5. **Real-time prior authorisation** — Abridge/Availity partnership (Jan 2026) auto-drafts prior auth from notes
6. **Nursing documentation** — DAX Copilot for Nurses (late 2025)
7. **Referral letter auto-generation** — Suki + Freed + Medwriter
8. **Cross-clinician review workflows** — 10% documentation time reduction with proper training (2025 systematic studies)
9. **Prompt injection awareness** — NHS England guidance (2025); red-teaming required for high-risk specialties
10. **Model transparency** — version tracking per document + changelog + upgrade approval emerging

## Australian regulatory (Feb 2026 TGA guidance)

- **Medical Device if:** AI generates diagnosis / differential / treatment recommendation NOT explicitly stated by practitioner
- **NOT Medical Device if:** scribe only records + summarises without analysis/interpretation
- Heidi, Lyrebird, Smart Scribe currently operate as non-device summaries
- My Health Record integration: growing push, requires APP 12 compliance + AU data residency
- RACGP: 22% of GPs use AI scribes (Mar 2025); endorses with caveat "GPs must verify; no replacement for human review"
- MBS: MBS coding supported by Smart Scribe + Heidi; no specific AI-scribe MBS rebate yet
- Compliance artefacts: SOC2, HITRUST, ISO 27001 (Augmedix, Heidi)
